Media Management

Social Media and Online Profile Management

What is Social Media Management?

Social media management is the process of analyzing social media data, audience, and other metrics to create a tailored media strategy to efficiently grow an organic and responsive following. There are many reasons one would want social media management, ranging from wanting to increase brand awareness, to build an online presence and portfolio, or even set up an online platform to communite with your customers.

My social media and online profile management covers platforms such as Instagram, FaceBook, Google MyBusiness, Yelp and more!

What to Expect?

With many years of experience managing online and social profiles, I've been able to narrow it down to a science and a repeatable method.

You can expect to see your media engagement and rankings increase withing a matter of a few weeks. My strategy relies heavily on high quality media that organically attracts attention, and carefully analytical planning to retain current following and reach new audiences.
